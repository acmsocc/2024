Women in Systems Meetup at SOCC

We are excited to host a Women in Systems meetup at SOCC this year! The broad goal of this meetup is to build an active and broad community, similar to WICARCH and N2Women. During the meetup, we will discuss potential approaches to building this community.


This meetup is also a space to connect with fellow women in the systems community, share experiences, and expand your professional circle. Women at all stages of their careers (students, postdocs, faculty, researchers, engineers) are welcome to attend.



Please feel free to grab your breakfast and join us for a morning of conversation, collaboration, and connection!


Where: TBD


When: Thursday, November 21, 2024, 8:00-9:00am



Please contact Neeraja J. Yadwadkar (neeraja@austin.utexas.edu) with any questions.
