ACM Symposium on Cloud Computing 2024

Seattle, CA

November 2024

{%endcapture%} {% include slider.html content=slider-title %}

SoCC'24 will take place on third week of November, in Seattle, WA. PC chairs Neeraja Yadwadkar and Peter Alvaro are finalizing the call for papers, and we will have more details soon.

Tentative paper deadline is July 15th, 2024!

SoCC'24 is 15th edition of the annual ACM Symposium on Cloud Computing, the premier conference on cloud computing. It brings together researchers, developers, users, and practitioners interested in cloud computing, and is the only conference co-sponsored by the ACM Special Interest Groups on Management of Data (SIGMOD) and on Operating Systems (SIGOPS). As in prior SoCC conferences, SoCC'24 will foster connections between the academic and industrial communities operating in the cloud space.

SoCC'24 will be an in-person event in Santa Cruz, CA from October 30th to November 1st, 2023.
